Breathe Easy - Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how "Breathe Easy" (the "App") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not directly collect any personal information. All data (breathing session history, language preferences) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

However, the following information may be automatically collected by third-party advertising services:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • Device information (device model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data

2. How We Use Information

  • Display advertisements within the App
  • Measure advertising performance
  • Provide personalised ads (with user consent)

3. Data Retention

The App does not retain any personal data on external servers. Advertising-related data retention is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob for advertising. For details on how Google collects and processes data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS 14.5 and later, the App requests your consent before tracking your activity. You may decline tracking without any impact on App functionality; non-personalised ads will be shown instead.

6. Your Rights

  • Deleting the App removes all locally stored data from your device.
  • You can change ad tracking permissions at any time in iOS Settings.
  • You can reset your advertising identifier to limit personalised ads.

8. Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this policy will be communicated through App updates or on this page.
