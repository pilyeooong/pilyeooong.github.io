Privacy Policy

App: Stroop Color Rush - Focus
Developer: pilyeooong
Effective date: May 18, 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile app Stroop Color Rush. The app is a casual Stroop color puzzle game and does not require account registration or sign-in.

2. Personal Information We Collect

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, photos, precise location, health data, or other directly identifying personal information.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores gameplay settings and history locally on your device, such as language choice, practice palette, accessibility settings, recent scores, accuracy, and reaction-time results. This local data is not sent to our server. Deleting the app removes this local data.

4. Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to show banner and interstitial ads. We configure the app without App Tracking Transparency tracking prompts and do not ask permission to track you across other companies' apps and websites.

Google may process device or ad-delivery information to provide, limit, secure, and measure ads according to Google's policies. Please review Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Purpose of Use

Local gameplay data is used only to display your settings, recent history, and best scores inside the app. Advertising-related processing is handled by Google AdMob for ad delivery, fraud prevention, frequency capping, and measurement.

6. Retention

Local gameplay data remains on your device until you delete the app or the app data. We do not maintain a separate server database for this app.

7. Third-Party Sharing and Processing

We do not sell personal information. Google AdMob is the third-party service used for advertising. Its handling of advertising-related data is governed by Google's terms and privacy policy.

8. Your Choices and Rights

You can delete locally stored app data by deleting the app from your device. You can also manage advertising preferences through your operating system and Google settings where available.

9. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us so we can respond appropriately.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: pilyeooong@gmail.com

11. Changes

We may update this policy when app features or legal requirements change. The latest version will be posted at this URL.
