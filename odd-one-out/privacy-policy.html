Privacy Policy for Find Odd One

Effective date: May 14, 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application “Find Odd One” provided by pilyeooong.

2. Personal Information We Collect

Find Odd One does not require account registration and does not directly collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location, contacts, photos, or other personal information.

The app stores gameplay records such as score, mistakes, grid size, accuracy, and play time locally on your device. These records are used only inside the app and are not transmitted to our server.

3. Advertising

Find Odd One uses Google AdMob to display banner and interstitial ads. AdMob may process device identifiers, approximate location, ad interaction data, diagnostics, and similar information according to Google’s policies. The app does not use App Tracking Transparency permission and does not request permission to track you across apps and websites.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Purpose of Use

  • To provide the puzzle test, practice mode, and local history features.
  • To display and measure advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • To maintain app stability and basic functionality.

5. Retention

Local gameplay records remain on your device until you delete the app or clear app data. Advertising-related data is handled by Google according to Google’s retention policies.

6. Third-Party Provision and Processing

We do not sell or provide personal information to third parties. Advertising data may be processed by Google AdMob as described above.

7. Your Rights

You may delete locally stored gameplay records by deleting the app or clearing its data on your device. For advertising data handled by Google, please refer to Google’s privacy controls.

8. Children

Find Odd One is a simple puzzle app and is not directed to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

9. Contact

Privacy contact: pilyeooong@gmail.com

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes. The effective date above will be updated when a material change is made.
