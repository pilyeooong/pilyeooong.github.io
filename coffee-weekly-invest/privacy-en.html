Coffee Weekly Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 28, 2026

Scope

This policy applies to the Coffee Weekly mobile app.

Data We Collect

Coffee Weekly does not require login and does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, precise location, payment information, brokerage account information, or trading information.

The app reads public stock/ETF and exchange-rate records from Supabase to display historical simulations. User inputs such as coffee amount, selected asset, period, and language are processed on device for app functionality.

Ads

The app may display non-personalized ads through Google AdMob. Google may process device and ad delivery information according to Google's policies. Learn more at Google Privacy Policy.

Third Parties

We do not sell personal information. We use Supabase for read-only data access and Google AdMob for advertising.

Retention

The app does not maintain app-owned user accounts or server-side personal profiles. Language preference may be stored locally on the device and can be changed by the user.

User Rights

You may delete the app to remove local app data from your device. Contact us for privacy questions.

Changes

We may update this policy when app functionality changes. The updated effective date will be shown on this page.
