Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

"Mystic Eight" (the "App") respects your privacy and complies with applicable laws. This policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require account registration or login. The following information is processed to provide the service:

  • Anonymous user identifier: A random ID automatically generated by Supabase anonymous authentication. It is not linked to your name, email, or any personal identifier, and is stored on your device.
  • Birth date and time: Entered by you. For paid readings, it is sent to our server to compute the BaZi chart and generate an AI interpretation. It is not stored on our servers.
  • Payment transaction ID: The transaction ID issued by Apple IAP after a successful purchase is stored on our server to prevent duplicate use.
  • Request timestamps: To enforce a rate limit of 20 requests per hour, the time of each request is recorded on our server.
  • Reading history: Your recent reading results are stored only in local storage on your device (AsyncStorage) and are never sent to our servers.

2. Purpose of Use

  • Computing the BaZi chart and generating AI fortune readings
  • Preventing duplicate use of paid transactions
  • Preventing service abuse (rate limiting)

3. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Used for anonymous authentication, transaction deduplication, and request logging. (Supabase Privacy Policy)
  • OpenAI: After a paid purchase, your BaZi data (abstracted from birth date/time — no personal identifiers) is sent to OpenAI to generate an AI reading. (OpenAI Privacy Policy)
  • Apple: In-app purchases are processed by Apple. (Apple Privacy Policy)

We do not sell or share your information with any other third parties.

4. Data Retention

  • Anonymous user ID and transaction IDs: Retained as long as needed for service purposes.
  • Request timestamps (rate limit): Automatically expire after 1 hour.
  • Device reading history: Deleted automatically when the app is uninstalled.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

You may delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the App. To request deletion of server-side data (anonymous ID, transaction records), please contact us at the address below.

7. Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this policy will be posted on this page.
