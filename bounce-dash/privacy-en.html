Privacy Policy

App: Bounce Dash · Effective date: July 22, 2026

Bounce Dash ("the App") respects your privacy. The App does not require sign-up or sign-in and does not collect directly identifiable information such as your name or email address.

1. Information We Collect

  • Anonymous leaderboard data: Your stage clear time and a random anonymous device identifier generated by the App. This identifier is not linked to an account or direct personal information and is not used to directly identify you.
  • Advertising data: Google AdMob may process device information, approximate location, ad impressions, clicks, and related advertising data to serve non-personalized ads. The App does not use App Tracking Transparency (ATT) and does not track you across apps or websites.
  • On-device data: Game progress, best times, language settings, and update prompt history are stored on your device.

2. How We Use Information

  • To calculate and display anonymous leaderboard rankings
  • To provide app functionality such as saved progress, language settings, and update prompts
  • To serve non-personalized ads and operate the App

3. Retention

Anonymous leaderboard data is retained while the service is operated. On-device data and the anonymous identifier are removed from your device when you delete the App.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: Provides non-personalized advertising. Google processes data according to the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: Infrastructure for storing and processing anonymous leaderboard data.

5. Your Rights

The App does not collect directly identifiable account information. You can remove on-device data by deleting the App. For privacy inquiries, contact the developer using the address below.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
