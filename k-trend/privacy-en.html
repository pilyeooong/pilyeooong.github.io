Privacy Policy

App Name: K-Trend - Korea Daily

Developer: pilyeooong

Last Updated: July 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

This app does not collect any personal information. No sign-up, login, or entry of your name, email, or contact details is required.

2. Local Data Storage

The app stores only the following data locally on your device (AsyncStorage). This data is never transmitted externally:

  • Selected language preference
  • Bookmarks (trend items you saved)

Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.

3. Content Retrieval

To display daily-updated Korea trend content, the app reads public content from the developer's server (Supabase) in a read-only manner. No personal or identifiable data is sent to the server during this process.

4. Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob for banner and interstitial ads, and requests non-personalized (contextual) ads only — meaning no cross-app user tracking is performed. AdMob may collect the following device-level data to serve ads:

  • Device advertising identifier
  • IP address, device type, operating system version
  • Approximate location based on IP
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected by Google and governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

This app does not use App Tracking Transparency and does not request tracking permission.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • Supabase — trend content delivery (read-only, no personal data sent)

7. Data Security

Because this app does not collect or transmit personal information, there is no risk of a personal-data breach on the developer's side.

8. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in accordance with applicable law or service changes. Any changes will be posted on this page.

9. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact pilyeooong@gmail.com.
